CONCLUSION

We survey in our area about people who are using different OTT platforms. As per our survey we finally conclude that many of the people are using at least one OTT platform. Amazon Prime Video and Netflix are the most used OTT platforms. Other platforms such as ETV Win (6%), ZEE5 (2%), Sony Liv (2%), and OTT Play (2%) have a smaller user base. Overall, the data highlights a growing inclination toward digital streaming, with the majority of users preferring platforms that offer a mix of affordability and diverse content.
